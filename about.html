About ShopWave

ShopWave is a modern and user-friendly e-commerce platform designed to provide fast, secure, and smooth online shopping experiences. Our platform allows customers to browse and purchase high-quality products with ease using well-optimized interfaces and modern technologies.

We built ShopWave with a strong focus on the Nepali market, enabling customers to make safe payments using eSewa and Khalti. Our goal is to keep online shopping simple, fast, and easy for everyone.

Our Mission

To make online shopping accessible, enjoyable, and secure for every user by offering a fast, well-designed, and affordable local e-commerce platform.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and leading Nepali e-commerce service focusing on convenience, transparent transactions, and innovative features like wallet top-up and smart product filtering.

Why Choose ShopWave?

✔ Easy login & fast checkout experience

✔ Secure payments using eSewa & Khalti

✔ Clean modern UI with smooth animations

✔ Better product sorting using Selection Sort algorithm

✔ Simple, fast and made specifically for Nepali users
