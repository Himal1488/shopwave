Our Services

  • Product Listing System: Products categorized by price, popularity, and features. Future categories planned.

  • eSewa & Khalti Payment Integration: Secure payments using leading Nepalese digital wallets. Sandbox testing supported for safe payment simulation.
  • Wallet Top-Up Feature: Users can load money into their ShopWave Wallet (via eSewa/Khalti) and use it for purchasing products.
  • Order Placement System: Smooth and fast checkout experience. Users can buy products using real payment or top-up balance.
  • Order History & Tracking: Users can review past orders and track their delivery status (future update planned).
  • Customer Support Integration: Contact form that sends messages directly to admin for support or issue reporting.
